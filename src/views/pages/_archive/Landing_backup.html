FAQ

Designed to answer the real questions finance and operators ask before they trust a system.

What makes this “2026-grade” instead of just “modern”? ⌄

Calm UX, inspectable governance, explainable workflows, and interactive storytelling baked into the product surface — not marketing gimmicks. It feels like an OS: policies behave consistently across modules.

Can we loosen controls (1-way / 2-way) when needed? ⌄

Yes. The goal is not maximal control — it’s intentional control. You can toggle posture by module, tenant, workflow stage, or time window. Every override can be stamped with “who/why” and evidence.

How do you prevent “audit theater”? ⌄

The system records behaviors, not claims: linked artifacts, decision rationale, and immutable events. Auditors don’t want slides. They want traceable evidence.

Does this replace our legacy system immediately? ⌄

Typically, teams run in parallel first: import, reconcile, and gradually move workflows over once confidence is high. The landing page demo panel above is modeled after that “stable migration” mindset.
