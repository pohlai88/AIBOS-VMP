Common Questions

How do I create a new case?

Navigate to the Case Dashboard and click "Create Case" to initiate a new dispute or inquiry.

How do I track payments?

Visit the Payments page to view your payment history, remittances, and download receipts.

How do I update my banking information?

Go to your Profile page and submit a bank details change request. Changes require approval.

How do I escalate an issue?

Contact your account manager or use the escalation path available in case details.
